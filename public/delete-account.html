Delete Account

You can request permanent deletion of your Arcway account and all associated data at any time.

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your account profile and authentication records
  • All registered Mac devices linked to your account
  • All session metadata associated with your account

This action is irreversible. Once deleted, you will need to sign in again to create a new account.

Request Account Deletion

Send us an email from the address associated with your account and we will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

Email Deletion Request

Opens your email app with a pre-filled deletion request. Make sure to send from the email address you used to sign in to Arcway.
